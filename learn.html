How to make Resin products

Lets make a Bookmark!

Materials Required
Plastic Cups2
Spatula3
Resin20ml
Hardener20ml
PigmentsUsed here:
  • Black
  • Gold flakes
Mouldbookmark

Step 1:

Always start out any resin project by wearing the right equipment. Using masks and gloves are mandatory. You may also additionally lay a plastic sheet on your work station.

Step 2:

Measure out the resin into cups as instructed on the bottle, here we are using 1:1 ratio. So we measure out one part resin and one part hardener and mix it togther gently to avoid many bubbles.

Step 3:

Set this mixture aside to allow any bubbles to rise up and pop.

Step 4:

Into cups, measure out how much resin you need of each color. Next we pour in the pigments into the mixture and mix gently.

  1. First, in one cup pour the resin mixture along with gold flakes.
  2. In another cup, pour the resin mixture with black resin pigment and mix gently. Make sure not to mix the spatulas being used.

Optional:

You can choose to set this aside for a while if any bubbles have been formed.

Step 5:

Making sure the mould is clean, pour the liquid resin in the desired pattern.

  1. Pour the gold flakes-resin on one end of the mould.
  2. Pour the black resin on the other end of the mould.
  3. Repeat this until the two pigments meet and the mould is filled.

Step 6:

Set this aside for the next 24 hours in a clean, dry place. Make sure the surface is level.

Step 7:

After 24 hours, you can unmould the piece carefully from the silicone mould.

Step 8:

Your one of a kind piece is ready to use!
